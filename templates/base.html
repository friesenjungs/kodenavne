Kodenavne
  • How to play Kodenavne!

    General

    Kodenavne is a game for two teams. There is one Spymaster and at least one Operative per team. It is played with a grid of 25 words, some of them assigned to one of the teams. Only the Spymaster can see which words belong to which team. The Spymasters take turns giving a clue to their Operative, trying to guide him to guess their team's words. The team that reveals all their words first wins.

    The Spymaster

    Each turn starts with the Spymaster giving a clue to his Operative. A clue consists of a word and the number of words in the team color that it relates to.

    The Operative

    The Operatives guess the team's words based on the Spymaster's clue. If the Operative guesses a word correctly, he may guess again. The goal is to guess as many words as the Spymaster has indicated. He can also guess unsolved clues from previous rounds until the maximum number of guesses (clue number + 1) is reached. The turn can end in different ways. If a word is revealed on a neutral card or in the opponent's color, the Operative may not give another Guess. The turn can also be ended manually or by reaching the maximum number of guesses.

    Winning and loosing

    A team wins when all the words of their own color have been guessed. They lose the game by revealing the black card.
